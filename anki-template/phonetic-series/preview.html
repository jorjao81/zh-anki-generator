Memory Aid

The phonetic component 青 (qīng) gives the sound to many characters. Notice how the pronunciation varies slightly: qīng, qíng, qǐng become jīng, jìng with different radicals. The meaning often relates to clarity, purity, or calmness—concepts associated with the color blue-green.
